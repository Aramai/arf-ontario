ARF Ontario is not a registered charity. ARF Ontario does not issue tax receipts.


All of our funds provide food, life saving medical care, training and supplies for dogs and cats. We strive to spend every penny on the high vest costs that rescued animals incur.

If you prefer to write a cheque payable to Strathroy Animal Clinic and mail to:

Strathroy Animal Clinic
425 Frances Street
Strathroy, Ontario
N7G 2L7
your cheque will be applied to the ARF Ontario account for rescure surgeries and required vet care.

Donated supply items mean more funds can be directed to medical care! The following are some items that are currently at the top of our wish list:

  • Puppy toys & collars (all sizes)
  • Kitty Litter!
  • New litter boxes
  • Canned dog food (adult and puppy)
  • Blankes and towels
  • Leashes and collars (for adult dogs of all sizes)
  • Baby gates
  • Kong toys
  • Canadian Tire money
  • Puppy exercise pens - available at pet supply stores and Canadian Tire

Many ARF rescues are orphaned puppies and kittens. The require extra special care and items:

  • Bottles of unflavoured Pedialyte liquid - available in the infant pharmacy aisle of most grocery or drugstores
  • Puppy or kitten formula (available at Global Pet Foods)
  • Baby oatmeal cereal, rice cereal, or pablum cereal
  • Stage one plastic baby bottles
  • Grocery store gift certficiates
  • Stuffed animals (without bean bag weights inside)
