How Does It Work?

Come and fall in love! Pet, snuggle, giggle and admire the beeeautiful (not to mention vet checked, microchipped, vaccinated, de-fleaed, and socialized!) dogs and cats. Then:

  • Complete an adoption application
  • Chat with an ARF volunteer during a telephone interview

For every person that chooses the adoption option instead of petshop purchase, you have SAVED A LIFE and another abandoned or abused animal can be saved by ARF. Join the thousands of happy homes that have opted to adopt.... tail wags and whisker twitches will be sent your way!
